Managed Service Programme

About the Managed Service Programme

What is the JobCrypt Managed Service Programme?

As a busy employer building the future of commerce and digital in Web3, the JobCrypt Managed Service Programme has been created to help you manage the challenging time constraints associated with acquiring the right talent. Be it co-founders or senior level staff, down to analysts and entry level Juniors, the JobCrypt Managed Service Programme will help find you the right individuals quickly and cost efficiently ensuring that your focus is where it needs to be i.e. getting your priorities done.

How it works

Our team will tailor your listing requirements to your needs and design a tailor made outreach campaign for your job opportunity. Once your job is listed on JobCrypt we will embark on this outreach campaign engaging the right communities and institutions to ensure appropriately qualified individuals apply to your role in the shortest time possible. During this period we carefully monitor the performance of your campaign ensuring that you recieve the right quantity of qualified applicants. At the end of the campaign period we provide you with a performance report, to support your further hiring decisions. Our team is on hand to support any requests you may have or optimisations that you may require during the campaign with the goal of helping you achieve your recruitment success.
