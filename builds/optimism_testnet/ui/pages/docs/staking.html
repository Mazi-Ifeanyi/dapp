Staking With JobCrypt

Why we use Staking?

Staking is an important part of the JobCrypt Job Seeker experience. The following details why we have included it.

  • Why Stake?

    Staking is necessary for JobCrypt's function as it enables JobCrypt to identify your wallet during the job application process. This protects the service from data harvesters, making sure that you applications are properly accounted for.

  • So I've staked then what?

    Once you have staked you should be able to apply for any Job posting on JobCrypt whilst your funds are staked.

  • What happens when I'm done applying?

    Once you have finished applying for roles that you are interested in on JobCrypt, you simply unstake your funds and they are returned to your wallet.

  • Does JobCrypt apply any interest?

    No.

We are open, trustless, permissionless and decentralized

Open

JobCrypt listings are open, they live on the blockchain and using Open Block EI, only you can modify your listing.

Trustless

The creation of listings on JobCrypt is completely trustless. As soon as you post your listing goes up right away.

Permissionless

Posting on JobCrypt is permissionless, post your job when you want, only you have access to your listing.

Decentralized

JobCrypt listings are 100% decentalized, we don't have any secret databases anywhere.

Why Choose JobCrypt

JobCrypt is the first decentralized Job Board hosted on Optimism. We bring employers and job seekers together using the power of blockchain to enable a faster, cleaner and more efficient hiring process.

Why we use Web 3?

Traditional job boards allow for inauthentic responses to employment listings, quite simply anyone can say for example "I know Web3" with no proof leading to difficult consequences for both job seekers and employers

Benefits for Employers

By using JobCrypt, employers are assured that their staff are fully competent in the basics of using Web3 as part of their daily operation saving on, training, time off and re-work.

Benefits for Job Seekers

By applying through JobCrypt, job seekers can clearly demonstrate that they are Web3 capable and worthy of clear and concise consideration by employers
