About The Conference

Blockchain technology has been gaining popularity in recent years and one of the key concerns surrounding its adoption is its sustainability. Blockchain sustainability refers to the environmental impact of the energy-intensive process of mining cryptocurrencies, which requires high computing power and consumes a significant amount of electricity.
